A Life in Motion: My Journey So Far

I was born on May 30, 2008, and from a young age, my life has been defined by movement. Growing up, my family relocated frequently, which meant I was constantly changing schools and adapting to new environments. While moving wasn't always easy, it became one of my greatest strengths. It exposed me to a wide variety of cultures, perspectives, and people, teaching me how to connect with anyone, anywhere.

My drive and ambition come directly from my parents. We started from very humble beginnings, but I watched my family work tirelessly to change our circumstances. Their resilience paid off, bringing us to the great place we are today. That journey taught me the ultimate lesson: hard work, consistency, and dedication can rewrite your future.

Through all the changing cities and schools, I always had a few constants that kept me grounded. One of the biggest was the world of *One Piece*. More than just a show, it taught me about the value of unwavering loyalty, chasing impossible dreams, and pushing past your limits. I carry that same 'pirate king' ambition into my coding, my gym routine, and my everyday life. I am always moving forward, always eager to learn, and always ready for the next adventure.
